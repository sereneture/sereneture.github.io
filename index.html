I am a Ph.D. candidate (2023–) in Management Science and Engineering (A+) at the College of Systems Engineering, National University of Defense Technology. I was selected into the 2025 CAST Young Talent Cultivation Program — Doctoral Track (recommended by the Chinese Institute of Command and Control), and was an outstanding trainee of the Young Talent Pilot Program — Chinese Association of Automation (CAA) Class. I visited the AI Department at Westlake University for half a year in 2025.

My research focuses on autonomous UAV swarm combat, multi-agent coordination, and evolutionary intelligence. I have rich experience in embedded electronic system design and algorithm development, and have independently built intelligent vehicles and UAV hardware platforms. I currently serve as a reviewer for IEEE Transactions on Evolutionary Computation. Recent interests include intelligent command & control, LLM agents, and embodied intelligent robots.

Outside research, I am an amateur marathon runner, a Tencent Campus Musician (QQ Music certified), and play several traditional Chinese instruments.
